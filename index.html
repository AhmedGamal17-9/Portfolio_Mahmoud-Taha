About Me

I’m Mahmoud Taha, an Arabic language teacher with over six years of experience teaching both Arabs and non-Arabs, including students with special educational needs.

My passion lies in creating interactive and meaningful learning experiences that connect to students’ goals and cultural backgrounds.

I hold a certified degree equivalency in the UAE and have taught in several reputable institutions under ADEK and the Ajman Educational Zone.

My goal is to inspire a love for the Arabic language and help learners communicate confidently in real-life situations.
